Ishpreet Sekhon

Designer and Programmer

I am a designer and programmer whose primary passion is to make games. I love designing and implementing unique gameplay mechanics that have a lot of expandability and depth to them. Some of the most rewarding work I've ever done is making games with friends that someone enjoyed. I have experience programming in a variety of languages, and consider myself an expert with Object Oriented Programming and Unity.

Other than games, I also really enjoy UX design and web development. In any design decisions that I make I always put the user first, and I have practice using many UX design tools that support user-centric design. This is especially true for my web development experience as accessibility and personalization are two important user-centric themes that I am constantly considering when designing and programming websites.
