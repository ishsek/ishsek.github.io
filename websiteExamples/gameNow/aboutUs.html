About

Who We Are

GameNow is a small group of passionate individuals dedicated to bringing games to everyone. We're showing the industry that streaming digital games is easier than ever, and the team here works to make sure the people that want to play games have the easiest access possible.

Our Promise

Our goal is to bring game streaming to the main stream as the primary method of playing games. Anyone with access to a web browser and control method would be able to play, regardless of system specs.

History

We are a recently formed team with experience in digital games distribution. Our team has many veterans of the games industry who have been working on games for over a decade.
